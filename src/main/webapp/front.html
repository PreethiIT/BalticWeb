Welcome to ArcticWeb

An effort from Danish Maritime Authority to improve maritime safety in the Arctic region. ArcticWeb serves as a single access point to safety related information, provides a streamlined way to do reporting to Arctic Command and it allows for voluntary coordinated voyage through schedule and route sharing. If you have a ship in the region, you may become a user via the button below.

Ice Charts

The Danish Meteorological Institute, Greenland Ice Service, collects information regarding ice conditions in the waters around Greenland and distributes this information to ships primarily as ice charts and reports.
